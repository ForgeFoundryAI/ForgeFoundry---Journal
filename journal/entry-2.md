# Journey Log - Entry #2
**Date:** April 21, 2026  
**Title:** Early Architecture & Tier Refinements

We spent time refining the core product structure and philosophy today.

Key decisions:
- Defined the three tiers clearly: Spark (Free), Forge ($39/mo), and Foundry ($99/mo), with Diamond Blueprints as a high-value one-time option.
- Positioned Crucible as the hidden “rocket engine” that powers all three phases of the journey.
- Began exploring the idea of three evolving layers inside Crucible (Discovery, Evolution, Completion).

We also clarified our guiding principle: “Build the machine while using the machine.” Every improvement we make while developing actual products should be folded back into Crucible to make the whole system stronger over time.

The rocket metaphor feels right — it gives the project an aspirational yet grounded feel. Progress is starting to feel real and structured.
