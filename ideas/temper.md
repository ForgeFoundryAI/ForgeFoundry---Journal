# Temper

**Polymarket Human-First Companion Tool**  
**Score:** 86.1/100 (Strong Foundry Level Candidate)

### Origin Story

Temper was discovered during a dedicated Spark scan focused on Polymarket pain points in April 2026.

While BidLite and ScopeGuard came from contractor communities, Temper emerged from heavy chatter in crypto/trading spaces — particularly around **execution fragility** for human traders competing against bots.

### Core Pain

Human traders on Polymarket frequently lose money not because of bad predictions, but due to platform issues:
- Ghost fills and orders that appear matched but never settle
- Nonce exploits and race conditions
- UI glitches, slippage, and positions disappearing
- Poor mobile experience during fast markets
- Difficulty competing with high-frequency bots that exploit timing gaps

Many traders describe the platform as “unreliable for real humans” despite loving the markets themselves.

### Evolution Context

Temper was strengthened by cross-pollination with BidGuard thinking:
- Just as contractors lose money *after* winning the bid (scope creep), traders lose money *during* execution (glitches and bot advantages).
- The Evolution Layer identified a clear pattern: **protection-focused companion tools** are highly valuable in environments where the underlying platform is imperfect or adversarial.

This reinforced our belief in building “shield” style tools that help humans thrive without forcing them to become bots themselves.

### Core Value Proposition

A lightweight, real-time companion (browser extension + mobile app) that acts as a **tempering shield** for human Polymarket traders:

- Live monitoring for ghost fills, nonce exploits, and execution anomalies
- Instant alerts + one-click “Safe Mode” before high-stakes trades
- Automatic logging with screenshots/timestamps for dispute protection
- Simple execution helpers and bot-activity indicators
- Clean mobile dashboard for active trading sessions

**Temper** helps you keep your cool and protect your edge — without turning you into a bot.
